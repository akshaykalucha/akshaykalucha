About Us

At Databricks AI, we’re a team of passionate engineers committed to pushing the boundaries of technology. With a focus on data-driven innovation, we specialize in delivering AI and ML solutions that empower businesses to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to build tools and teams that solve real-world problems. Today, we’ve grown into a network of over 5,000 consultants, operating hubs in Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Chennai, and serving clients across the US and India. Our engineers bring expertise from industry leaders like Google, Microsoft, and Amazon, ensuring top-tier solutions every time.

We believe in relationships over transactions. Our Contract-to-Hire model and referral-driven talent acquisition reflect our dedication to long-term partnerships, resulting in consultant tenures averaging over 3 years and client relationships lasting 2+ years.
